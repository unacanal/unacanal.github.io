Research

My research focuses on addressing fundamental low-level vision challenges, particularly in Terahertz (THz) imaging. Achieving high-quality THz image reconstruction and enhancement is a challenging task due to the unique characteristics of THz images, such as low signal-to-noise ratio and limited bandwidth. My research goal is to find an image restoration method that works well on THz images and apply it to other frequency bands (Infrared, even visible light).
